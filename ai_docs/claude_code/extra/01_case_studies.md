## Enterprise Case Studies

### **Novo Nordisk (Pharmaceutical)**
Novo Nordisk, maker of Ozempic, faced a massive documentation bottleneck where clinical study reports run 300 pages and staff writers averaged just 2.3 reports annually. With Claude Code, the team expanded their NovoScribe tool beyond clinical study reports to device protocols and patient materials, **generating complete study booklets in under a minute**—work that previously took months with external agencies. Their 11-person development team avoided scaling up while dramatically expanding capabilities, with even non-technical team members prototyping features in hours instead of weeks.

### **Newfront (Insurance Brokerage)**
This leading U.S. insurance brokerage implemented Claude to automate HR support, contract review, and internal knowledge management, achieving:
- HR teams reclaim **over one month per year** in administrative time
- **60% reduction in document-processing costs** across departments

### **TELUS (Technology Company)**
Engineering teams report a **30% improvement in code delivery velocity**. TELUS processes over **100 billion tokens per month**, demonstrating Claude's scalability for large-scale enterprise deployments.

### **IG Group (Financial Services)**
Analytics teams now save **70 hours weekly**, redirecting that capacity toward higher-value strategic work. In certain use cases, **productivity doubled**, and the company hit **full ROI within three months**.

## Best Practices & Key Insights from Practitioners

### **Mental Model**
Multiple developers emphasize thinking of Claude Code as "a very fast intern with perfect memory" rather than a tool—eager to help, incredibly capable, but still needs clear direction and supervision.

### **Productivity Patterns**
- **Specificity matters**: Success rates improve significantly with more specific instructions
- **Research-Plan-Execute pattern**: Asking Claude to research and plan before coding significantly improves performance
- **Context management**: Use CLAUDE.md for background knowledge so Claude remembers preferences
- **Collaboration over automation**: Active guidance yields better results than full auto-accept mode

### **Real-World Performance**
From multiple sources:
- **2x productivity** on common development tasks
- What took "an entire day now completes in a few hours"
- Best when "you're already an expert who knows what to build and how"
- Turns "boring parts like boilerplate, unit tests, documentation into quick prompts"

### **Usage Evolution**
Data shows developers increasingly delegate more complex work requiring less oversight over time, with average task complexity and maximum consecutive tool calls increasing.

## Practical Takeaways

1. **ROI Timeline**: IG Group achieved full ROI in 3 months; expect significant productivity gains within weeks
2. **Budget Planning**: Plan for $1000-1500/month for senior engineers using AI extensively
3. **Team Velocity**: Expect 30-67% improvements in code delivery metrics
4. **Skill Level Impact**: Works best for experienced developers who can guide and review effectively
5. **Code Quality**: Engineers must remain responsible for AI-generated code; implement rigorous code reviews
6. **Learning Curve**: Develop best practices through experimentation; mental models matter
7. **Scale**: Successfully deployed from solo developers to 100+ billion token/month enterprise operations
